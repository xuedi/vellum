Rust & Roll - Official Band Page

About

Rust & Roll is a four-piece alternative rock band formed in Berlin in 2019. Known for their energetic live performances and lyrics about software development, the band has carved out a unique niche in the tech music scene.

Members

Compiler Chris - Lead Vocals & Guitar

The founder and frontman of Rust & Roll. Chris started coding at age 12 and picked up guitar at 15. His unique vocal style combines melodic verses with screamed choruses about memory safety.

  • Real Name: Christian Mueller
  • Instruments: Vocals, Electric Guitar, Acoustic Guitar
  • Influences: Foo Fighters, System of a Down, The Prodigy

Borrow Betty - Bass & Backing Vocals

Betty brings the low end with thunderous bass lines inspired by prog rock and electronic music. Her background in music theory gives the band its complex time signatures.

  • Real Name: Elisabeth Braun
  • Instruments: Bass, Synth, Backing Vocals
  • Influences: Tool, Muse, Daft Punk

Async Andy - Lead Guitar

The shredder of the group, Andy is known for his lightning-fast solos and innovative use of guitar effects. He builds his own pedals in his spare time.

  • Real Name: Andreas Schmidt
  • Instruments: Lead Guitar, Effects
  • Influences: Van Halen, Buckethead, Plini

Panic Pete - Drums & Percussion

Pete's powerful drumming drives the band's intense sound. A former jazz drummer, he brings technical precision with punk rock energy.

  • Real Name: Peter Hoffman
  • Instruments: Drums, Percussion, Electronic Pads
  • Influences: Neil Peart, Dave Grohl, Jojo Mayer
